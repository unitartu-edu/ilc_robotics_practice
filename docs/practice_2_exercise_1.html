P2 Exercise 1 - Introduction¶

Note

Practice 2 is optional. You don’t have to do it if you don’t feel like it. But its way more challenging and hence fun

In this practice we are going to kick the difficulty up a notch and switch the simulation environment. The simulation itself is based on Webots simulator that is hosted on robotbenchmark.net. Robotbenchmark has created a list of challenges, where the fist two (“Viewpoint Control” and “Robot Programming”) just introduce the environment. So let’s have a look.
